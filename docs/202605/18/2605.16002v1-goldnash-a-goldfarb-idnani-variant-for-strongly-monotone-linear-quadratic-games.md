## 摘要
我们提出了一种 Goldfarb-Idnani (GI) 对偶有效集法的简单变体，用于计算具有凸二次代价以及共享仿射不等式和等式约束的强单调 N 玩家博弈的变分广义纳什均衡。我们证明，尽管博弈的联合 KKT 系统中可能存在非对称伪梯度矩阵，该算法仍保持了其原始性质。我们的数值结果表明，这种被称为 GoldNash 的方法在大多数情况下优于其他现有的最先进算法，并能实现博弈论线性模型预测控制律的高效计算。该算法的 Python 实现可在开源 NashOpt 库中获得。

## Abstract
We present a simple variant of the Goldfarb-Idnani (GI) dual active-set method for computing variational generalized Nash equilibria of strongly monotone N-player games with convex quadratic costs and shared affine inequality and equality constraints. We prove that the original properties of the algorithm are maintained in spite of having a possibly non-symmetric pseudogradient matrix in the joint KKT system of the game. Our numerical results show that the method, which we call GoldNash, most often outperforms alternative state-of-the-art algorithms and enables very efficient computations of game-theoretic linear model predictive control laws. A Python implementation of the algorithm is available in the open-source NashOpt library.
