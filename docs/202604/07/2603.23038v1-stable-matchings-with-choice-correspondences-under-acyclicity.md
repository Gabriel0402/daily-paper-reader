## 摘要
我们研究了当主体具有选择对应而非偏好关系时稳定匹配的存在性。我们通过弱化路径独立性（Path Independence）假设，扩展了 Chambers 和 Yenmez (2017) 的框架。对于多对多市场，我们证明了当选择对应满足替代性（Substitutability）和一种新的广义非循环性（General Acyclicity）条件时，稳定匹配是存在的。我们利用“增长或舍弃算法”（Grow or Discard Algorithm）提供了一个构造性证明，该算法通过迭代地扩展或消除合同，直到达到一个强极大个人理性（Individually Rational）集合。我们提供了一种获取稳定匹配的算法，其中被拒绝的合同不会被永久舍弃，这使我们的方法与标准的延迟接受算法（DAA）类型算法有显著区别。对于一对一市场，我们证明了仅凭路径独立性并不能保证稳定性。我们引入了一种基于替换的稳定性概念，并提供了一种在选择对应满足二元非循环性（Binary Acyclicity）时构造稳定匹配的算法。JEL 分类：C62, C78, D01, D47。关键词：选择对应，替代性，广义非循环性，多对多匹配，带合同的匹配，增长或舍弃算法，替换稳定性，二元非循环性。

## Abstract
We study the existence of stable matchings when agents have choice correspondences instead of preference relations. We extend the framework of Chambers and Yenmez (2017) by weakening the Path Independence assumption. For many-to-many markets, we show that stable matchings exist when choice correspondences satisfy Substitutability and a new General Acyclicity condition. We provide a constructive proof using a Grow or Discard Algorithm that iteratively expands or eliminates contracts until a strongly maximal Individually Rational set is reached. We provide an algorithm to obtain stable matchings in which rejected contracts are not permanently discarded, distinguishing our approach significantly from standard DAA-type algorithms. For one-to-one markets, we show that Path Independence alone does not guarantee stability. We introduce a replacement-based notion of stability and provide an algorithm that constructs stable matchings when choice correspondences satisfy Binary Acyclicity.
